Percy Jackson

The son of Poseidon. Percy can control and manipulate water, allowing him to create powerful waves and control water currents. Percy possesses enhanced physical abilities, making him stronger and faster than an average human. Percy has the ability to communicate with and control sea creatures. He is also an excellent swordsman.

Annabeth Chase

The daughter of Athena. Annabeth is extremely intelligent and possesses vast knowledge on a variety of subjects, including architecture, strategy, and mythology. She is a strategic thinker, able to analyze and anticipate her opponent's moves.

Grover Underwood

A satyr, which is a half-man, half-goat. Grover has a strong connection to nature and can use his abilities to communicate with and control plants and animals. He has heightened senses of smell, hearing, and sight. Grover has a strong sense of empathy and can sense the emotions of those around him. He is a skilled musician and often uses his music to soothe or inspire others.

The Author: Rick Riordian

Rick Riordan is an American author who is best known for his works in the fantasy and young adult fiction genres. He was born on June 5, 1964, in San Antonio, Texas, and began his writing career as a middle school English and social studies teacher. His love of mythology and his experience teaching inspired him to write his first series, Percy Jackson and the Olympians, which became a massive success and has since been adapted into films and a musical.
